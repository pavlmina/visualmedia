Hi there, Paul here
Visual Media Designer & Developer

Passionate about art from the moment I first picked up a pen, my journey into graphic design began with the humble brush tool in Windows 95 Paint.

With a degree in Information Technology, I developed a deep interest in multimedia and graphic design, shaping it into my career post-college and develop software design.

Over three years, I honed my skills in diverse roles—from production advertising to digital marketing design—working on projects ranging from retail merchandising to corporate branding and beyond.

I'm here in this field because of the opportunity to continuously learn and evolve, not only as an artist but also as an individual, through collaboration and showcasing my work.
